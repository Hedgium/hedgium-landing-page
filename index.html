Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and platform

What is algorithmic trading and how does it work?

Algorithmic trading uses computer programs to execute trades based on predefined criteria and mathematical models. Our algorithms analyze market data in real-time to identify high-probability trading opportunities and execute strategies with precision and speed that's difficult to achieve manually.

Do I need prior trading experience to use your platform?

No, our platform is designed to guide investors at all experience levels. We provide educational resources, clear strategy explanations, and a user-friendly interface that makes sophisticated trading accessible to everyone, regardless of their prior experience.

How do you manage risk in your trading strategies?

Risk management is at the core of everything we do. Our strategies incorporate multiple layers of protection including position sizing limits, stop-loss mechanisms, portfolio diversification, and real-time monitoring. We prioritize capital preservation while seeking consistent returns.

What kind of returns can I expect from your strategies?

While past performance doesn't guarantee future results, our strategies are designed to generate consistent risk-adjusted returns. The actual returns depend on market conditions, strategy selection, and portfolio size. We focus on sustainable returns rather than chasing high-risk opportunities.

How do I get started with Hedgium?

Getting started is simple: choose a subscription plan that matches your portfolio size, complete the onboarding process, connect your trading account (we support major brokers), and start exploring our strategy recommendations. Our team is available to assist you throughout the setup process.

Can I use Hedgium with my existing broker?

We currently integrate with most major brokers in India. During the onboarding process, you'll be able to check if your broker is supported. If you don't see your broker listed, contact our support team as we're continuously adding new integrations.
